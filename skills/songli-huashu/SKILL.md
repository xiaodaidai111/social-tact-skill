# 送礼话术

Use this skill to make gift-giving sound thoughtful rather than transactional.

## Formula

```text
礼物来意 + 对对方的心意/感谢 + 降低负担 + 简短收尾
```

## Before Writing

Analyze the gift, relationship, risk of looking transactional, and the other person's personality:

- Practical person: explain usefulness briefly.
- Elder or teacher: emphasize respect and心意, not price.
- Client or leader: be extra careful about propriety.
- Close friend: keep it warm and relaxed.

## Default Output

1. `简短版`
2. `正式版`
3. `亲近版`
4. `分寸提醒`

## Rules

- Do not imply exchange of benefits.
- Do not make the gift sound expensive or burdensome.
- With leaders, teachers, and clients, be extra careful about propriety.
- For friends and family, emphasize thoughtfulness.
- If the gift may be inappropriate, warn the user.
